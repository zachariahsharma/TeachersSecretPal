VHS PTSA Secret Pals

Welcome to the Vandegrift PTSA sponsored Secret Pal program! The VHS Secret Pal Program is the most fun and exciting staff appreciation program found anywhere around. Our parent pals (or their student) drop off a surprise at the front desk for their Vandegrift secret pal once a month starting in October -- school supplies, treats such as cookies or flowers, lunch, etc. Your identity is not revealed until our Reveal Party at the end of the school year! Sign ups for parents will begin on the first day of school, be sure to sign up before August 31st! You will have the choice of the number of Secret Pals you wish to have! Then join the VHS Secret Pals Facebook group to receive updates and share ideas!
